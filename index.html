03How I got here

It started at Kendriya Vidyalaya Pattom, Shift 2 — a gold medal in the school-level maths olympiad, a bronze in English, and a perfect 10 CGPA in the 2016 CBSE Class X boards.

Then bio-maths for Class XI–XII, where I co-designed a disabled-friendly toilet for a Science Congress competition — my first taste of engineering built around actual human needs. Finished with 96% in the boards, second at school level, wrote both NEET and KEAM, and chose circuits.

At SCT College of Engineering I took Electronics & Communication and graduated with an 8.9 CGPA — plus a Minor in Machine Learning (the plot twist this whole page is about) and Honours in MEMS.
